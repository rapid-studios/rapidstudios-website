## Comparison Scope

- Comparison variable:
  - overall atmosphere

- Held constant:
  - typography hierarchy
  - component language
  - CTA model
  - motion posture
  - selective density approach

## Light Direction

- Strengths:
  - calmer and more editorial
  - clearer by default
  - lower risk of drifting into generic SaaS styling
  - easier to keep premium with placeholder proof content

- Risks:
  - slightly less dramatic at first glance
  - may feel more familiar if the team wants a bolder presence

## Dark Alternative

- Strengths:
  - more cinematic and contrast-led
  - feels sharper and more assertive
  - can make the brand feel more visibly designed at first glance

- Risks:
  - easier to become generic blue-on-black SaaS
  - requires stricter control over glow, border contrast, and accent intensity
  - motion and hover states need more restraint to avoid feeling theatrical

## Recommendation

- Recommendation:
  - Review both in Figma before approving gate 5.
  - Do not switch the whole system to dark unless the dark board still feels restrained, premium, and studio-grade once viewed side by side with the light version.

## Approval

- Decision: Approved
- Notes: Gate 5 approved on 2026-03-06 with the dark alternative selected as the visual lock for the v3 cycle.
